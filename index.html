Headquartered in Austin, Texas, Arete Choice is an operating family office with over $100 million in equity capital. We acquire majority stakes in high-quality companies who believe in a people-centric culture. Our purpose is to acquire and enhance the legacy of family-owned businesses by caring about employees and making long-term investments.

What we do

Arete acquires majority stakes in businesses that generate $1-10 million in annual pretax free cash flow. We seek to close transactions reliably and efficiently. We partner with the founders of family-owned companies to create a customized transition of ownership and/or leadership. Arete takes pride in collaborating with operators, caring for employees, and reinvesting cash flows into future opportunities. We invest our own capital. We charge zero fees of any kind – no management fees paid by investors, no monitoring fees paid by our companies, no incentive fees paid on gains (in fact, we would be delighted to own the company forever).

Our mission

Arete Choice is committed to creating a win for everyone involved in the ownership transition and ongoing operation of family-owned companies. That means:

  • Provide liquidity to current shareholders by selling a majority stake to new ownership that never intends to sell.
  • Foster an environment of trust, caring, learning, and growth for all employees.
  • Deepen and expand relationships with customers and suppliers.
  • Make a positive difference for our communities, country, and world.

Our beliefs:

  • Business can be a powerful lever for good. It’s why we’re here.
  • People matter. Everyone can thrive in an environment that encourages excellence.
  • In leadership that empowers and recognizes employees.
  • That fulfilling jobs at high quality companies have a positive impact on individuals, marriages, and families.
  • In everyday heroes – hard working people who are the backbones of our communities.
  • In creating a win for everyone involved: legacy shareholders, employees, customers, suppliers, and community.
  • That Small and medium-sized businesses are a critical component of the US economy.
  • A business does not need to be in technology or artificial intelligence or cryptocurrency to create value and make a real difference.
  • A culture of excellence is a competitive advantage and a rewarding way to live. We take pride in superb execution and superior results.

What does Arete Choice mean?

Arete means "excellence of any kind". In ancient Greece, this excellence was tied to the fulfillment of purpose or function – the act of living up to one's full potential. A person of Arete uses all of their faculties — strength, bravery, and wit — to achieve real results. Therefore, Arete involves every aspect of a human’s ability and potential. It is based on a settled disposition of the mind, which relies on principle to choose one’s actions and emotions.
